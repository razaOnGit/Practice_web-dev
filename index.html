Mussoorie Trip

Mussoorie is a popular hill station in India, located in the northern state of Uttarakhand. It is known for its scenic beauty, pleasant weather, and colonial architecture. The town is situated at an altitude of approximately 2,000 meters (6,600 feet) above sea level and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
